Machines

Browse for a TM or HM

In Pokemon, a Machine is a disc that you can use to teach a new move to your Pokemon (don't ask how - no one knows how this makes sense).

A TM or Technical Machine is a move that can be taught and then replaced by another, whereas an HM or Hidden Machine cannot be forgotten other than through a Move Deleter. They also usually need a gym badge to be able to used outside of battle to overcome obstacles like cutting down trees or moving boulders.

Try type in the name of a TM or HM move that you'd want to find out about. If you're not sure, you can browse the entire library of 95 TMs and 6 HMs .
